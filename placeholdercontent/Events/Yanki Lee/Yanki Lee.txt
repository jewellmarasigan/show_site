Dr Yanki Lee joined the Helen Hamlyn Centre for Design in 2000 as a Research Associate, studying the future of live-work buildings in collaboration with the Peabody Trust. With an MA in Architecture from the Royal College of Art and a PhD in design participation from Hong Kong Polytechnic University, Yanki focuses on design methodology for participation and social innovation. She was co-investigator of the i~design project and the EPSRC funded Public Engagement Project, 'Design Our Tomorrow (DOT)' which introduced inclusive design methodology to UK secondary school Design & Technology curriculum and holder of the UK's BIS UK - China Fellowship of Excellence 2011.Yanki was awarded an RCA Fellowship in 2012.
